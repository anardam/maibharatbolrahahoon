<h2>The Digital Revolution</h2>

<p>India is the world's fastest-digitizing nation. Billions of transactions happen every month via UPI. Aadhaar has given a digital identity to 1.4 billion people.</p>

<h3>UPI — The World's Wonder</h3>

<p>Launched in 2016, UPI is today the world's largest real-time payment system. From tea stalls to shopping malls, QR code payments are everywhere.</p>
