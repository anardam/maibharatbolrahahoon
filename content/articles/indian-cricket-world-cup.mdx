<h2>Cricket — India's Religion</h2>

<p>Cricket in India is not just a sport — it's a way of life. In 1983, under Kapil Dev's captaincy, India won their first-ever World Cup and made history.</p>

<h3>1983 — Where It All Began</h3>

<p>Defeating the mighty West Indies at Lord's, India stunned the cricketing world. Kapil Dev's legendary 175-run innings remains etched in history.</p>

<h3>2011 — Victory at Home</h3>

<p>After a 28-year wait, MS Dhoni's iconic six at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium created history. For Sachin Tendulkar, this was the most special moment of his career.</p>
