<h2>Ayurveda — The Science of Life</h2>

<p>Ayurveda is India's most ancient healing system. 'Ayur' means life and 'Veda' means knowledge. It is based on the balance of body, mind, and soul.</p>

<h3>Core Principles</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Three doshas — Vata, Pitta, Kapha</li>
  <li>Treatment using natural herbs</li>
  <li>Importance of yoga and meditation</li>
  <li>Focus on diet and daily routine</li>
</ul>

<p>Kerala is considered the hub of Ayurveda. People from all over the world come here for Panchakarma and other Ayurvedic treatments.</p>
