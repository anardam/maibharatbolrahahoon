<h2>Rajasthan — Land of Kings</h2>

<p>Rajasthan is India's largest state, and its forts, palaces, and havelis are living witnesses to Indian history.</p>

<h3>Iconic Forts</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Amber Fort, Jaipur</strong> — A masterpiece of Rajput architecture</li>
  <li><strong>Mehrangarh, Jodhpur</strong> — A magnificent fort built on a 125-meter cliff</li>
  <li><strong>Chittorgarh</strong> — India's largest fort</li>
  <li><strong>City Palace, Udaipur</strong> — The jewel of the City of Lakes</li>
</ul>
